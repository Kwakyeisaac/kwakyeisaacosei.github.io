Project Overview

The School Management System (SMS) is specifically designed for basic schools in Ghana. It helps manage various operations such as student records, class attendance, fee payments, and academic performance based on the Ghana Education Service (GES) curriculum. The system supports efficient school management while ensuring compliance with GES regulations.

Features of the Ghana SMS

  • **Student Records Management**: Manage student information, from primary to junior high school (JHS).
  • **Attendance Tracking**: Monitor and log daily attendance, including tracking reasons for absenteeism, in line with the GES guidelines.
  • **BECE Preparation**: Tools to help track students' academic performance as they prepare for the Basic Education Certificate Examination (BECE).
  • **Grading System**: Supports the official Ghana Education Service grading system, enabling teachers to input grades and generate end-of-term reports.
  • **Fee Management**: Track school fees, payments, and outstanding balances, with automated reminders to parents or guardians.
  • **Timetable Management**: Efficient class scheduling and teacher assessment, including special programs for subjects such as Creative Arts and Design, English Language, Mathematics and Science.
